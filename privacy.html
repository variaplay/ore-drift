Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Ore Drift ("the game") is operated by Variaplay LLP. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices. The short version: we do not run any game servers, there are no accounts, and nothing about your play is sent to us.

No accounts, no sign-in

There is no account system. You play anonymously. You do not provide an email address, a real name, or any other identifier.

Data stored on your device

The game uses your browser's localStorage to remember two small preferences so you don't have to set them every session:

  • oredrift.playerName — the pilot name you type on the title screen, so it's pre-filled next time.
  • oredrift.muted — whether you muted the sound ("1" or "0").

These values stay on your device. They are never transmitted to us or to any third party. Clearing your browser's site data for variaplay.com removes them.

Data we do not collect

  • No email, phone, or account identifiers.
  • No game scores, play history, or session recordings.
  • No analytics cookies or tracking pixels.
  • No advertising cookies.
  • No uploads or user-generated content.

Third-party services

The game runs entirely in your browser. Static files are served by GitHub Pages, which may log standard web request metadata (IP address, user agent, timestamp) in its access logs; see GitHub's privacy statement for details. The Phaser game engine is loaded from the jsDelivr CDN, which operates under its own privacy policy. We do not receive access logs from either provider.

Audio and microphone

The game synthesizes its own music and sound effects using the browser's Web Audio API. It never requests microphone or camera access.

Children

The game is intended for general audiences. Because we do not collect any personal information, the game is suitable for all ages.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
